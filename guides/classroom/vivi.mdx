## Vivi Digital Signage Overview

**Vivi** is the digital signage solution used throughout SAS for displaying information, announcements, and content on screens in hallways, common areas, and central locations around campus.

<Note>
  **Campus-Wide Communication**: Vivi displays provide real-time information, announcements, schedules, and emergency notifications across the SAS campus.
</Note>

## Content Management (Staff)

### Publishing to Vivi Displays

<Note>
  **Staff Access**: Authorized staff can publish content to Vivi displays. Content is moderated to ensure appropriate, professional messaging.
</Note>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Who Can Publish">
    **Authorized Users:**

    - **Communications Team** - Primary content managers
    - **Administrators** - School-wide announcements
    - **Department Heads** - Department-specific content
    - **Club Advisors** - Club announcements (with approval)
    - **EdTech Team** - Technical content management

    **Need Access?**
    - Contact Communications Office
    - Request Vivi publishing permissions
    - Complete content guidelines training
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="How to Publish">
    **Publishing Content:**

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Access Vivi Portal">
        Log in to Vivi content management system with SAS credentials
      </Step>

      <Step title="Create Content">
        - Upload images, videos, or text announcements
        - Choose template or design layout
        - Add information, dates, times
      </Step>

      <Step title="Select Display Zones">
        Choose which displays to show content:
        - All campus
        - Specific buildings
        - Targeted areas (e.g., cafeteria only)
      </Step>

      <Step title="Schedule Display">
        - Choose start and end dates
        - Set display times (all day or specific hours)
        - Set rotation frequency
      </Step>

      <Step title="Submit for Approval">
        Content is reviewed before publishing (for student safety and appropriateness)
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      **Best Practice**: Submit content 2-3 days in advance for review and scheduling.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Content Guidelines">
    **What to Include:**

    ✅ **Clear, concise messaging** - Easy to read from a distance

    ✅ **High-resolution images** - Looks professional on large screens

    ✅ **School branding** - Use SAS colors and logos

    ✅ **Important dates/times** - When, where, who

    ✅ **Contact information** - How to learn more

    **What to Avoid:**

    ❌ **Too much text** - Keep it brief and scannable

    ❌ **Small fonts** - Must be readable from 10+ feet away

    ❌ **Unmoderated student content** - Requires adult review

    ❌ **Personal information** - Privacy concerns

    ❌ **Commercial advertising** - Educational content only
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Vivi in Classrooms

### Classroom Vivi Integration

Some classrooms may have Vivi in addition to standard AV equipment:

**Classroom Uses:**
- **Wireless screen sharing** - Similar to Apple TV
- **Display student work** - Share from any device
- **Collaboration** - Multiple students share simultaneously
- **Annotations** - Draw/markup on shared content
- **Content capture** - Record and save lessons

<Info>
  **Vivi vs Apple TV**: While both support wireless sharing, Vivi offers additional collaboration features. Check with EdTech about which system your classroom uses.
</Info>

### Classroom Sharing with Vivi

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Share from iPad/Mac">
    **Wireless Screen Sharing:**

    1. **Install Vivi app** (if required for your classroom)
    2. **Connect to SAS WiFi**
    3. **Open Vivi app** or use AirPlay to Vivi receiver
    4. **Enter room code** displayed on classroom screen
    5. **Start sharing** - Your screen appears on display

    **Or use built-in AirPlay** if enabled
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Multiple Presenters">
    **Collaborative Sharing:**

    **Vivi Advantage**: Multiple students can share to the same screen

    1. Each student connects to Vivi
    2. Up to 4 devices can share simultaneously
    3. Teacher controls which screens to display
    4. Split-screen or rotate between presenters

    **Great for**: Group presentations, student collaboration, peer feedback
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Display shows 'No Signal' or blank screen" icon="display-slash">
    **Display not showing content:**

    1. **Check power:**
       - Verify display is powered on
       - Look for LED indicator light
    2. **Check connections:**
       - Vivi receiver connected to display
       - HDMI cable secure
       - Network cable connected
    3. **Wait for refresh:**
       - Content may be updating
       - Can take 1-2 minutes to refresh
    4. **Check Vivi box:**
       - Vivi receiver should have lights indicating power/network

    **Still blank?** Report to [ithelp@sas.edu.sg](mailto:ithelp@sas.edu.sg)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Content is outdated or not updating" icon="clock-rotate-left">
    **Stale content on display:**

    1. **Check time/date:**
       - Content may be scheduled for different time
       - Display shows content based on schedule
    2. **Network connectivity:**
       - Vivi needs network to pull new content
       - Check if WiFi/network is working
    3. **Content not published:**
       - Verify content was approved and published
       - Check Vivi portal for status
    4. **Cache issue:**
       - Vivi may need restart to clear cache

    **Contact**: Communications Office or [edtech@sas.edu.sg](mailto:edtech@sas.edu.sg)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't connect to Vivi from classroom" icon="wifi-slash">
    **Wireless sharing issues:**

    1. **Verify WiFi connection:**
       - Must be on SAS network
       - Check WiFi is working properly
    2. **Check room code:**
       - Ensure you're entering correct code from display
       - Code should be visible on screen
    3. **Vivi app installed:**
       - Some systems require Vivi app
       - Download from Self Service (JAMF) or App Store
    4. **Alternative sharing:**
       - Try AirPlay if available
       - Use HDMI cable as backup

    **Need help?** [edtech@sas.edu.sg](mailto:edtech@sas.edu.sg)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Inappropriate content on display" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    **Report content concerns immediately:**

    <Warning>
      If you see inappropriate, offensive, or incorrect content on a Vivi display, report it immediately.
    </Warning>

    **How to Report:**
    1. **Note location** - Which display/building
    2. **Take photo** if possible (for reference)
    3. **Contact immediately:**
       - Communications Office
       - Administration
       - [edtech@sas.edu.sg](mailto:edtech@sas.edu.sg)
    4. **Urgent issues:** Contact IT to disable display immediately

    **Content is moderated**: All content should be reviewed before publishing. Errors or inappropriate content should be rare but must be reported.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Requesting Vivi Publishing Access

### How to Get Access

<Steps>
  <Step title="Determine Need">
    Identify why you need to publish to Vivi:
    - Department announcements
    - Club/activity promotion
    - Event publicity
    - Student recognition
  </Step>

  <Step title="Contact Communications">
    Email Communications Office or [edtech@sas.edu.sg](mailto:edtech@sas.edu.sg):
    - Explain your role and need for access
    - Specify which display zones you need
    - Describe type of content you'll publish
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Training">
    Attend Vivi content creation training:
    - Learn content guidelines
    - Practice creating announcements
    - Understand approval workflow
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive Credentials">
    Get Vivi portal login credentials and start publishing!
  </Step>
</Steps>
