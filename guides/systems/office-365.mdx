## Office 365 Overview

All SAS staff have access to **Microsoft Office 365**, with **Word, Excel, and PowerPoint** available for use. Other Office 365 applications are restricted and require special approval.

<Note>
  **All staff members** receive Office 365 licenses with access to Word, Excel, and PowerPoint (desktop and web apps). OneDrive and SharePoint are **not available**. Microsoft Teams requires approval from Central Admin.
</Note>

<Warning>
  **Permitted Apps Only:** Only Word, Excel, and PowerPoint are permitted. OneDrive and SharePoint are disabled. Teams is available only upon request to Central Admin.
</Warning>

## Storage for Office Files

### No OneDrive or SharePoint

<Warning>
  **Important:** OneDrive and SharePoint are **not available** at SAS. All file storage must use **Google Drive**.
</Warning>

**For Office 365 files:**
- ✅ Save Word, Excel, PowerPoint files to **Google Drive**
- ✅ Use Google Drive for Desktop to sync files
- ✅ Store in Shared Drives for team collaboration
- ❌ OneDrive is disabled and not accessible
- ❌ SharePoint is not available

**Saving Office Files to Google Drive:**

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Desktop Apps">
    **Save to Google Drive from Office:**

    1. Install Google Drive for Desktop
    2. Open Word, Excel, or PowerPoint
    3. Click **File → Save As**
    4. Navigate to **Google Drive** in save dialog
    5. Choose location (My Drive or Shared Drive)
    6. Click **Save**

    Files automatically sync to cloud!
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Web Apps">
    **Office Online:**

    When using Word/Excel/PowerPoint online at office.com:
    1. Files save to temporary browser storage
    2. Download completed files
    3. Upload to Google Drive manually

    **Better option:** Use Google Docs/Sheets/Slides for web-based editing
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Info>
  **File Compatibility:** Office files (.docx, .xlsx, .pptx) work perfectly in Google Drive. You can open them with desktop Office apps while they're stored in Drive.
</Info>

## When to Use Office 365

### Best Use Cases

**Use Office 365 when you need:**
- ✅ Advanced Excel features (macros, pivot tables, data analysis)
- ✅ Professional Word formatting (complex documents, templates)
- ✅ Offline work with desktop applications
- ✅ Compatibility with external Office documents

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't sign in to Office 365" icon="circle-exclamation">
    **Login issues:**

    1. Use your **full email:** username@sas.edu.sg
    2. Use your **SAS password** (same as Google/WiFi)
    3. Clear browser cache and cookies
    4. Try different browser
    5. Contact IT if account is not activated

    **New staff:** Account may take 24-48 hours to activate
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Office asking for product key" icon="key">
    **Activation issues:**

    **You should NOT need a product key!**

    1. Sign out of Office completely
    2. Sign back in with @sas.edu.sg email
    3. Office should activate automatically
    4. If persists, contact IT to verify license

    **IT will:** Check license assignment in admin portal
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't install on personal device" icon="download">
    **Installation problems:**

    1. Verify you're staff member (students don't have Office 365)
    2. Sign in at office.com first
    3. Check you haven't exceeded device limit (5 devices)
    4. Download installer directly from office.com
    5. Contact IT if issues persist

    **Not seeing Install button?** Account may not have license
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
