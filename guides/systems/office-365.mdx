## Accessing Office 365

### Sign In Methods

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Office Portal">
    Visit [office.com](https://office.com) or [office365.com](https://office365.com)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign In">
    **Email:** username@sas.edu.sg

    **Password:** Your SAS password
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Apps">
    Click the app launcher (⊞) to open Word, Excel, PowerPoint, etc.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Desktop Applications

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Windows">
    **On SAS Windows Computers:**

    - Office apps pre-installed
    - Already activated with your account
    - Open from Start menu
    - Auto-sign in with Windows credentials

    **Applications:**
    - Word, Excel, PowerPoint
    - Outlook, OneNote, Teams
    - Publisher, Access (select computers)
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="macOS">
    **On SAS MacBooks:**

    **Download Office:**
    1. Go to [office.com](https://office.com)
    2. Sign in with @sas.edu.sg
    3. Click **Install Office**
    4. Download and run installer
    5. Sign in to activate

    **Or use Self Service (JAMF):**
    - Open Self Service app
    - Search for "Microsoft Office"
    - Click Install
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Personal Devices">
    **Install on Your Own Computer:**

    Staff can install Office 365 on personal computers:

    1. Sign in at [office.com](https://office.com)
    2. Click **Install Office**
    3. Choose **Office 365 apps**
    4. Download and install
    5. Sign in with @sas.edu.sg to activate

    **License includes:**
    - 5 PC/Mac installations
    - 5 tablet/iPad installations
    - 5 phone installations
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## When to Use Office 365

### Best Use Cases

**Use Office 365 when you need:**
- ✅ Advanced Excel features (macros, pivot tables, data analysis)
- ✅ Professional Word formatting (complex documents, templates)
- ✅ Offline work with desktop applications
- ✅ Compatibility with external Office documents
- ✅ Department requires specific Office tools

**Use Google Workspace when you need:**
- ✅ Real-time collaboration with multiple people
- ✅ Quick document sharing and editing
- ✅ Cross-device access (iPad, Chromebook, etc.)
- ✅ Integration with ClassLink and SAS systems
- ✅ Unlimited storage and sharing

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't sign in to Office 365" icon="circle-exclamation">
    **Login issues:**

    1. Use your **full email:** username@sas.edu.sg
    2. Use your **SAS password** (same as Google/WiFi)
    3. Clear browser cache and cookies
    4. Try different browser
    5. Contact IT if account is not activated

    **New staff:** Account may take 24-48 hours to activate
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Office asking for product key" icon="key">
    **Activation issues:**

    **You should NOT need a product key!**

    1. Sign out of Office completely
    2. Sign back in with @sas.edu.sg email
    3. Office should activate automatically
    4. If persists, contact IT to verify license

    **IT will:** Check license assignment in admin portal
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't install on personal device" icon="download">
    **Installation problems:**

    1. Verify you're staff member (students don't have Office 365)
    2. Sign in at office.com first
    3. Check you haven't exceeded device limit (5 devices)
    4. Download installer directly from office.com
    5. Contact IT if issues persist

    **Not seeing Install button?** Account may not have license
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
