## Email Account Overview

Singapore American School provides email accounts through **Google Workspace** for students and staff members.

<Note>
  All SAS email addresses use the format: **username@sas.edu.sg**
</Note>

## Accessing Your Email

### Web Browser (Any Device)

1. Go to [mail.google.com](https://mail.google.com)
2. Sign in with your full email: `username@sas.edu.sg`
3. Enter your SAS password

### Gmail App (iPad/Mobile)

1. Open the **Gmail app**
2. Tap **Add account** → **Google**
3. Enter: `username@sas.edu.sg`
4. Enter your SAS password
5. Tap **Next** to complete setup

### Mail App (MacBook)

1. Open **Mail** application
2. Go to **Mail** → **Add Account**
3. Select **Google**
4. Enter: `username@sas.edu.sg`
5. Enter your SAS password
6. Choose which features to sync (Mail, Contacts, Calendars)

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I forgot my password" icon="key">
    **For Students:**
    - Contact your teacher or visit the IT Help Desk
    - Parents can email [ithelp@sas.edu.sg](mailto:ithelp@sas.edu.sg)

    **For Staff:**
    - Use the password reset portal (link provided by IT)
    - Contact [ithelp@sas.edu.sg](mailto:ithelp@sas.edu.sg) for assistance
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't access my email" icon="circle-exclamation">
    **Check these common issues:**
    1. Are you using the full email address? (`username@sas.edu.sg`)
    2. Is your password correct?
    3. Are you connected to WiFi or internet?
    4. Try clearing browser cache or using a different browser

    **Still having issues?** Contact [ithelp@sas.edu.sg](mailto:ithelp@sas.edu.sg)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Email not syncing on my device" icon="rotate">
    **Try these steps:**
    1. Check your internet connection
    2. Remove and re-add the account
    3. Restart your device
    4. Check device storage (low storage can prevent syncing)

    **Need help?** Contact [ithelp@sas.edu.sg](mailto:ithelp@sas.edu.sg)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
