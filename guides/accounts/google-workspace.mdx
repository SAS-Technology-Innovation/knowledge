## What is Google Workspace?

Singapore American School uses **Google Workspace for Education** as our primary productivity platform. This includes Gmail, Google Drive, Docs, Sheets, Slides, and many other collaborative tools.

<Note>
  **All SAS users** (students from Grade 3+ and all staff) have access to Google Workspace with their @sas.edu.sg account.
</Note>

## Signing In to Google

### Your Google Credentials

<Steps>
  <Step title="Email Address">
    Use your **full SAS email address**

    Format: `username@sas.edu.sg`

    Example: `edtech@sas.edu.sg`
  </Step>

  <Step title="Password">
    Use your **SAS password** (same password for WiFi and ClassLink)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  **Important**: When signing into Google, you MUST use your full email address including `@sas.edu.sg`
</Warning>

### Access Google Workspace

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Browser">
    **From any device:**

    1. Go to [google.com](https://google.com)
    2. Click **Sign In** (top right)
    3. Enter: `username@sas.edu.sg`
    4. Enter your SAS password
    5. Click **Next**

    **Direct Links:**
    - Gmail: [mail.google.com](https://mail.google.com)
    - Drive: [drive.google.com](https://drive.google.com)
    - Classroom: [classroom.google.com](https://classroom.google.com)
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="ClassLink (Easiest)">
    **Automatic sign-in:**

    1. Open [ClassLink LaunchPad](https://launchpad.classlink.com)
    2. Sign in with your SAS username
    3. Click any Google app (Gmail, Drive, Docs, etc.)
    4. **You're automatically signed in!**

    <Tip>
      Using ClassLink is the easiest way to access Google - no password needed!
    </Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Mobile Apps">
    **On iPad or Phone:**

    1. Download the Google app (Gmail, Drive, Docs, etc.)
    2. Open the app
    3. Tap **Sign In** or **Add Account**
    4. Select **Google**
    5. Enter: `username@sas.edu.sg`
    6. Enter your SAS password
    7. Follow the prompts to complete setup
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Real-Time Collaboration

One of the most powerful features of Google Workspace is **real-time collaboration**:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Share Your Document">
    Click **Share** button ‚Üí Enter email addresses or get shareable link
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Permissions">
    Choose access level:
    - **Viewer** - Can only view
    - **Commenter** - Can view and comment
    - **Editor** - Can make changes
  </Step>

  <Step title="Collaborate Together">
    Multiple people can work on the same document simultaneously. See each other's cursors and changes in real-time!
  </Step>

  <Step title="Version History">
    See all changes ‚Üí **File** ‚Üí **Version History** ‚Üí View and restore previous versions
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  **Group Projects Made Easy**: Share a folder with your team, and everyone can access all project files!
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't sign in to Google" icon="circle-exclamation">
    **Check these issues:**

    1. ‚úì Are you using your FULL email? (`username@sas.edu.sg`)
    2. ‚úì Is your password correct?
    3. ‚úì Are you trying to use a personal Google account instead?
    4. ‚úì Clear browser cache and cookies
    5. ‚úì Try a different browser

    **Still stuck?** Contact [ithelp@sas.edu.sg](mailto:ithelp@sas.edu.sg)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Files won't save or sync" icon="cloud-slash">
    **Troubleshooting steps:**

    1. Check internet connection
    2. Look for "Saving..." or "All changes saved" message
    3. Refresh the page
    4. Check storage space in Drive
    5. Try offline mode then reconnect

    **Need help?** Email [ithelp@sas.edu.sg](mailto:ithelp@sas.edu.sg)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't access a shared file" icon="lock">
    **Check access permissions:**

    1. Ask the owner to check sharing settings
    2. Make sure you're signed in with SAS account
    3. Request access using the "Request Access" button
    4. Verify the link is correct

    **Permission issues?** Contact the file owner or [edtech@sas.edu.sg](mailto:edtech@sas.edu.sg)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
