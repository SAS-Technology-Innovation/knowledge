## Welcome to The Eagle Way of Technology

The SAS Technology Knowledge Base is the main support portal for education technology at Singapore American School. Find guides, how-tos, and information about technology, systems, applications, and AI tools used throughout our community.

<Note>
  **The Eagle Way** - We're committed to providing you with the resources and support you need to succeed with technology at SAS.
</Note>

## Getting Help

Need assistance? We have two support teams ready to help you.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card
    title="EdTech Support (Level 0-1)"
    icon="chalkboard-user"
    color="#1A4190"
    href="mailto:edtech@sas.edu.sg"
  >
    **Education Technology Team**

    Contact for:
    - Classroom technology & instructional tools
    - Learning management systems (LMS)
    - Educational apps & platforms
    - ClassLink, PowerSchool, Veracross
    - Teaching & learning technology

    Email: [edtech@sas.edu.sg](mailto:edtech@sas.edu.sg)
  </Card>

  <Card
    title="IT Support (Level 1-2)"
    icon="laptop"
    color="#E51322"
    href="mailto:ithelp@sas.edu.sg"
  >
    **IT Help Desk**

    Contact for:
    - Device issues & hardware support
    - Network & connectivity problems
    - Account access & passwords
    - SAP & administrative systems
    - Infrastructure & technical issues

    Email: [ithelp@sas.edu.sg](mailto:ithelp@sas.edu.sg)
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  **Not sure which team to contact?** Start with EdTech for classroom and learning-related issues, or IT Support for device and system problems. We'll route you to the right team if needed.
</Tip>
